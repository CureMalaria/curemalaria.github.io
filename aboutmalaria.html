About Malaria

*NOT MEDICAL ADVICE* IF YOU HAVE MALARIA, SEEK MEDICAL TREATMENT FROM A DOCTOR

How do you get infected?

You can get infected with malaria when someone with malaria gets bitten by specific kinds of mosquitos, and that mosquito then bites you.

Symptoms

You normally get typical flu symptoms, although complications include:

  • Malaria infecting your brain, causing death
  • Accumulated fluid in your lungs (pulmonary edema)
  • Organ Failure
  • Anemia

Prevention

Malaria can be prevented by stopping mosquito bites. Using bed nets, wearing bug spray and taking steps to stop mosquito breeding can help.

How many people get malaria?

Aproximately 300 to 500 million new cases and between 1.5 and 2.7 deaths annually!
